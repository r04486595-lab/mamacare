# MamaCare - Maternal Health Tokens 🗂️

MamaCare is a revolutionary blockchain-based incentive system that rewards expectant mothers for attending crucial antenatal checkups and following proper maternal health protocols. Built on the Stacks blockchain using Clarity smart contracts, MamaCare tokenizes maternal healthcare engagement to improve health outcomes for both mothers and babies.

## 🌟 Overview

The MamaCare system addresses critical challenges in maternal healthcare by providing tangible incentives for expectant mothers to maintain regular healthcare visits, complete essential screenings, and follow medical recommendations throughout their pregnancy journey.

### Key Problem Addressed
- Low attendance rates for antenatal checkups in underserved communities
- Lack of engagement with preventive maternal health services
- Difficulty in tracking and incentivizing consistent healthcare behaviors
- Economic barriers preventing regular healthcare visits

## 🏥 System Architecture

MamaCare consists of two main smart contracts working together to create a comprehensive maternal health incentive ecosystem:

### 1. Health Records Contract (`health-records.clar`)
- **Patient Registration**: Secure registration system for expectant mothers
- **Medical Provider Management**: Certified healthcare provider verification
- **Checkup Recording**: Immutable record of completed antenatal visits
- **Milestone Tracking**: Progress monitoring through pregnancy stages
- **Health Score Calculation**: Comprehensive assessment of maternal health engagement

### 2. Token Rewards Contract (`token-rewards.clar`)
- **Token Minting & Distribution**: Automated reward distribution for health milestones
- **Incentive Tiers**: Multi-level reward system based on engagement consistency
- **Redemption System**: Token exchange for healthcare services, supplies, or cash benefits
- **Performance Analytics**: Detailed tracking of program effectiveness
- **Community Leaderboards**: Gamification elements to encourage participation

## 🎯 Health Incentives Structure

### Checkup Milestones
- **First Trimester Visits** (Weeks 4-12): 50 MAMA tokens per visit
- **Second Trimester Visits** (Weeks 13-26): 75 MAMA tokens per visit  
- **Third Trimester Visits** (Weeks 27-40): 100 MAMA tokens per visit
- **Essential Screenings**: Bonus tokens for completing required tests
- **Educational Sessions**: Rewards for attending health education classes

### Consistency Bonuses
- **Monthly Perfect Attendance**: 200 bonus MAMA tokens
- **Complete Program Participation**: 1,000 bonus MAMA tokens
- **Early Registration**: 150 bonus tokens for enrollment before 12 weeks
- **Referral Rewards**: 100 tokens for each successful referral

## 📊 Technical Specifications

### Blockchain Infrastructure
- **Platform**: Stacks blockchain for security and Bitcoin finality
- **Language**: Clarity smart contracts for safety and predictability
- **Consensus**: Proof of Transfer (PoX) for energy efficiency
- **Scalability**: Layer-2 solutions for high-volume transactions

### Token Economics
- **Total Supply**: Dynamic minting based on program participation
- **Distribution**: 70% patient rewards, 20% provider incentives, 10% administration
- **Redemption Rate**: 1 MAMA token = $1 USD equivalent value
- **Burn Mechanism**: Used tokens removed from circulation to maintain value
